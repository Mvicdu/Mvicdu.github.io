监视RMAN作业进度

2019-08-21

监视RMAN作业进度:
SQL>select opname,context,sofar,totalwork, round(sofar/totalwork*100,2) "%_complete" from v$session_longops
where opname like 'RMAN%'
and opname not like '%aggregate'
and totalwork !=0
and sofar <> totalwork;
